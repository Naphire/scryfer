The domestic dog is a canid which has been selectively bred for millennia for behaviors, sensory capabilities, and physical attributes.

Much companion. Many friendly.

Wolf

The grey wolf also known as the timber wolf, or western wolf, is a canid native to the wilderness and remote areas of North America and Eurasia.

Howls to communicate with the pack.
Lobster

Clawed lobsters comprise a family of large marine crustaceans. They have long bodies with muscular tails, and live in crevices or burrows on the sea floor.

Commonly boiled alive and eaten by humans.
